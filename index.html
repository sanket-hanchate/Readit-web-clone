How Can We Help You?

Whether you're experiencing issues or just have questions, we are here to assist you. Explore the resources below or reach out to us directly for personalized support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions from our users.

Q. How do I create an account on Readit?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button on the login page. Fill in your details like name, email, and password. Once registered, you will have access to personalized book recommendations and can save books to your library.

Q. I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your email address, and we’ll send you instructions to reset your password.

Q. How can I find trending books?

On the homepage, you’ll find a “Trending Books” section that showcases the most popular and trending books across various genres. You can also use the search bar to find specific books or genres.

Q. Can I access my library on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access your library from any device once you are logged in. Whether you’re on a desktop, tablet, or mobile, your library syncs automatically, allowing you to read from anywhere.

Q. How do I add books to my library?

To add a book to your library, simply click on the "Add to Library" button located on the book's details page. The book will be saved to your personal library for easy access later.

Q. Can I download books for offline reading?

At this time, Readit does not support offline reading. However, you can bookmark books to easily find them when you’re online.

Q. What genres of books are available?

We offer a wide range of genres, including fiction, non-fiction, mystery, fantasy, science fiction, romance, self-help, biographies, and more. Use the "Browse by Genre" section in the navigation to explore books based on your interests.

Q. How do I contact customer support?

If you need any assistance, you can reach out to our support team by clicking the "Contact Us" link located at the bottom of the website. You can also find help through our support email or live chat for faster responses.
